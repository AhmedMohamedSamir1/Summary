<< Match >>

  =MATCH(lookup_value, lookup_array, [match_type])
	- It tells you where in a list a certain value appears (its row or position number).

  --- Example
	A (Name)	B (ID)
	  Ahmed		  1
	  Amr		  2
	  Omer		  3

    =MATCH("Amr", A2:A4, 0) // Result = 2 , Because Amr is the 2nd item in the range A2:A4
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
